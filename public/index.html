What are the common symptoms of diabetes?
Common symptoms of diabetes include:

• Increased thirst and frequent urination
• Unexplained weight loss
• Fatigue and weakness
• Blurred vision
• Slow-healing sores
• Frequent infections

Note: These symptoms can vary between Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes. Always consult a healthcare professional for diagnosis.
